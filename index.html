About Mangata & Gallo

Founded by Mariana, a diamond-cutting and metal-smithing specialist, Mangata and Gallo brings timeless design to Austin and the world. Each piece is meticulously crafted with passion and precision.

Operating both online and in a boutique storefront with an atelier in Austin, Texas, Mangata and Gallo caters to life's most precious moments — from engagements to anniversaries.

With a growing team of artisans and a fresh presence on social media, Mariana continues to shape the future of luxury jewelry — one handcrafted piece at a time.
