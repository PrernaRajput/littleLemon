About Little Lemon

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant known for its blend of traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisines, offering a curated seasonal menu of 12–15 dishes that highlight fresh, bold flavors.

The restaurant provides a rustic and relaxed atmosphere at moderate prices, making it a popular destination for any time of the day—from casual lunches to cozy dinners.

Founded by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, Little Lemon reflects their shared passion for food and hospitality. Mario, the head chef, brings his experience from Italy and family recipes to the kitchen, while Adrian leads the restaurant’s marketing and expansion into broader Mediterranean fare.
